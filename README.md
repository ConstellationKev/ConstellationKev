# 💫 About Me:
Hi! My name is Kunzhong Wang and I am an engineering/robotics enthusiast from California! Although I am mainly a software guy, I love cadding, 3d printing, and electronics. My favorite project I made is a custom braille translator from irl text to dynamic braille using computer vision! I'm currently working on a custom coaxial helicopter that you can control with your hand! I'm also learning aviation physics, fractional order PID control. Talk to me about OpenCV, anything computer vision related, FIRST Robotics, coding, straykids, 3d printing, CAD!

In my free time, I love playing soccer, playing the clarinet and eating :D My favorite food combo is a costco hot dog and then in n out fries. I am also a huge cat person.
